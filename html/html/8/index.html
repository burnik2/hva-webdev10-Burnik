About Me

Hello Everyone This is Burnik and right now you are viewing my portfolio. I am dedicated and results-oriented computer science student with a strong foundation in mathematics. Currently pursuing a Master's in Computer Application at the University of Engineering and Management, Kolkata, I am eager to apply my technical skills to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions.

With a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and a consistent academic record throughout my schooling, I possess a strong analytical mindset and problem-solving abilities. My proficiency in programming languages like Python, Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, coupled with my ongoing exploration of SQL, positions me as a versatile developer capable of tackling diverse projects.

I am passionate about learning new technologies and expanding my skill set. My experience in developing projects such as a portfolio, to-do list, and a blog using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript demonstrates my ability to bring ideas to life through code.

Beyond academics, I enjoy playing chess, video games, and reading story books. These hobbies foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity, qualities essential for success in the tech industry.

I am eager to contribute my skills and knowledge to a dynamic team and embark on a fulfilling career in the field of computer science.
